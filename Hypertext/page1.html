The Nightmare

You're outside, parkouring across and climbing skyscrapers.
Your friend and several other climbers are there right beside you,

cheering

and

hollering

as they race to climb the farthest.
All of a sudden, the skyscraper you and your friend are on begins to

sway violently

and several people slip off.

Do you:
  1. Hold on to the nearest ledge with an iron grip.
  2. Try to catch a falling climber with one hand.
  3. Jump down onto a nearby balcony for protection.
